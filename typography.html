Driving While Black

Driving While Black

This project was inspired by the book Driving While Black by Gretchen Sorin. Each chapter of this book focuses on a specific challenge Black Americans faced. Chapter 8 focused on bombings in different locations that happened during the civil rights movement. Using imagery talked about in this chapter, I redesigned the opening chapter page to give an idea of what would be discussed.

Mr. Loverman

Mr. Loverman

Typographic systems compose the way we layout our designs and make them work for us. To practice these systems, I took one of my favorite songs and used the lyrics to create different depictions of each system. Each version also includes the tempo, rhythm, spacing of the lyrics as the song plays.
