I'm Hail O'Donnell (they/them). I grew up in the electric city of Schenectady, New York amongst a small community of queer and transgender individuals. I attended college at SUNY Cobleskill, a rural agricultural town outside of what I am used to, and received my BS in December of 2021. I have always had an eye for the weird and interesting.

I have a passion for representing minority groups of people with my design, whether it be with illustration, photography, web design, or even pottery. I also have a deep connection with goth, punk, and alternative communities. If you feel like my style fits your needs, feel free to contact me and tell me your cool ideas.
