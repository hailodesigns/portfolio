True Self

True Self was a project a I very passionate about. This project focused on the creation and branding of a non-profit LGBTQ+ resource center for underprivileged individuals. It was focused in Schenectady county on Jay street. The resource centers main goals was to spread awareness on the issues of accesibility withing the LGBTQ+ community, provide iclusive sex education for LGBTQ+ youth, and allow individuals to learn and become their true self.

SuperB

Ribbit Press

Ribbit Press

Ribbit Press is a froggy printing book that teaches you the different processes that go into printing physical material. This project was used to teach me these ideas in a way that I would remember by allowing us to focus on a specific theme. My choice of theme was frogs.

Phocycle Food Truck

Phocycle

Phocycle is an all organic and vegan food truck that visits local farmers markets around Albany county. This project allowed me to work from a template to design a vinyl for a food truck, something that is not commonly thought about when it comes to design.
