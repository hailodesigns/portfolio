Hong Kong

This travel poster for Hong Kong was created with the combined use of Photoshop and Illustrator. I created a mock-up of sampled tourist photography taken in Hong Kong to create a dream tourist location. Each shape was then individually drawn and colored to create this unique poster.

Tutoring Poster

Tutoring Poster

When I was hired on as a graphic design tutor at SUNY Cobleskill I was instructed to create a poster for myself to advertise the hours I would be avaliable for tutoring. This project allowed me to add a lot of my own personality into it. One of the first facts people learn about me is that I own parrots. This poster is an illustration of my Goffin's Cockatoo Pearl, an animal everyone gets to learn about when they are introduced to me.

Chocola Obscura

Chocola Obscura

Chocola Obscura was a very fun project. I was tasked with designing a logo and packaging for a chocolate bar brand. I went with a vintage magic show design/theme that sells fruit flavored chocolate bars. I did research on vintage magic show posters and vintage poster type to get the correct feel for the design.
