About our facilities...

Out of the Wild is an exotic and wildlife animal rescue with three different locations in the United States. We provide the rehabilitation and re-homing of each neglected exotic animals, such as reptiles, bird, and uncommon house pets, while also caring for the wildlife that is brought in by kindhearted individuals. Our establishment actively works along side vet facilities to provide the proper medical treatment for injured animals in hopes to rehome or release.


Our Beliefs

Continuous Learning Learning and adapting to an animals behavior requires persistence and dedication. We believe that we must continue to learn about our rescues in order to understand their needs and how we can help them find their forever home.
Ethical and Professional Our community pushes us to act in the best interest of the animals we care for and to strengthen the human-animal bond. Our staff is faced with difficult decisions each day and do their best to make the most ethical and professional decisions that are in the best interest of the animals well-being.
Teamwork In order to work against the increasing number of animals abandoned and mistreated each day we have to work as a team to spread the correct information for animal husbandry and proper care, It takes many hands to help an animal get back to thriving.
Community We rely on our community to report and mistreatment or injured animals that may need help. Our community allows us to care for wildlife and get them back to nature.
Passion In order to take proper care for the animals we take in, our team members have a drive and passion for animal husbandry and strive to give the best treatment to formerly suffering animals.
